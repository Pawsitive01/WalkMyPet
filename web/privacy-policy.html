Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

WalkMyPet ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile photo when you register.
  • Pet information: Name, breed, age, and photos of your pet(s).
  • Location data: GPS location during active walks to track routes (only when the app is in use).
  • Payment information: Processed securely via Stripe. We do not store your card details.
  • Communications: Messages sent between pet owners and walkers within the app.
  • Usage data: App activity, device type, and crash reports to improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the WalkMyPet service.
  • To connect pet owners with dog walkers.
  • To process bookings and payments.
  • To send booking confirmations and notifications.
  • To improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following cases:

  • Between users: Profile information is shared between pet owners and walkers to facilitate bookings.
  • Service providers: Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage; Stripe for payment processing.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Location Data

Location is only collected during active walk sessions and with your explicit permission. You can revoke location access at any time via your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

WalkMyPet is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections and secure cloud storage via Firebase.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.

10. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly within the app:

  1. Open the WalkMyPet app
  2. Go to your Profile
  3. Tap the menu (⋮) in the top right
  4. Select Delete Account and confirm

This will permanently delete your profile, pets, bookings, reviews, and all personal data. If you are unable to access the app, you may also request deletion by emailing us at support@walkmypet.app.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@walkmypet.app
